// A SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
- Let's anyone check the recent news and articles.
- Let's students log in, check their information, tuition and make payments.
- Let's Professors check their information, and the students.
- Let's the University check their information, list of all students and professors, total revenue, costs and profit.

// Students
- See full name, student ID and grade.
- Check tuition (paid/owed).
- Make a payment.

// Professors
- See full name, professor ID and Salary.
- See name of course.
- See Students list.

// University
- See Student and Professor lists.
- see total tuition paid.
- see all costs (salaries) that need to be paid.
